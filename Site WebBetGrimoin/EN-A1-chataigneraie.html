La Chataigneraie Project

• CLIENT: SESAME AUTISM ASSOCIATION, 18000 BOURGES
• ARCHITECT: CARRE D'ARCHE WORKSHOP, 18000 BOURGES


Engineering: JM GRIMOIN BET Fluid engineering services for Electricity - Smoke extraction - Heating - Ventilation - Air Conditioning - Plumbing
Additional services:
- RT 2005 CALCULATIONS
- HPE LABEL
Plumbing, heating, ventilation, and cooling for the restructuring of La Chataigneraie in OSMOY (18390).

The Project in Brief:
The purpose of this project is to define the necessary work for the implementation of:
· Sanitary appliance installations.
· Hot and cold water distribution installations.
· Wastewater disposal installations.
· Heat distribution installations.
· Heat emission installations.
· Mechanical ventilation installations.
· Cooling installations.
· Electrical connections, protection, control, and interlocking of the equipment installed under this lot.
