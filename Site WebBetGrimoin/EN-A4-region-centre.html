Central Region Project

• CLIENT: CENTRAL REGION COUNCIL 9 ST. PIERRE LENTIN STREET 45 041 ORLEANS CEDEX 1
• CO-CONTRACTOR: CYTHELIA LA MAISON ZEN 350 ROUTE DE LA TRAVERSE 73000 MONTAGNOLE


Engineering: JM GRIMOIN BET Technical, Legal, and Financial Assistance Mission for the Implementation of Photovoltaic Solar Panel Installation Plan in 29 High Schools in the Central Region





The project in brief:
The project of the CENTRAL REGION, named "Installation Plan of Photovoltaic Solar Panels in 29 High Schools," aims to leverage the expertise of operators in the sector to design, build, and operate investments that improve the energy performance of buildings. The mission entrusted to the contractor is to assist and support the CENTRAL REGION on a technical, legal, and financial level throughout the project in order to successfully conclude the necessary contract(s) for the project's realization.
