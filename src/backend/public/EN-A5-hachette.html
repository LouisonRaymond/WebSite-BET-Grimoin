Hachette Livre Project

• CLIENT: HACHETTE LIVRE 43 QUAI DE GRENELLE 75 905 PARIS CEDEX 15
• PROMOTER: ABCD IMMOBILIER 12 RUE GODOT DE MAUROY 15 009 PARIS


Engineering: JM GRIMOIN BET Detailed Study for Plumbing with Calculations and Equipment Sizing (Including Solar Panels), Opening and Reservations Plans, Execution Plans, and Schematic Diagrams. Coordination of various systems.





The project in brief:
Hachette Livre has a business building that houses some of its services in the town of Vanves. Since the building required significant renovation work, the client decided to construct a new commercial building on the site, which will serve as its future headquarters. This new building will include 850 office workstations and related facilities, as well as an employee cafeteria. The design of the building is optimized to make the best use of the parcel and to fit into its urban and architectural surroundings. HQE® certification, awarded by CSTB, is being pursued.
