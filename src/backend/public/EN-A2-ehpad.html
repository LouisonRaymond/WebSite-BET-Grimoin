Ehpad Project

• CLIENT: EHPAD 7 rue des Ecoles 58270 ST BENIN D'AZY
• ARCHITECT: PASCAL MALLARD ARCHITECT 15 RUE LOUIS VICAT BAT. 4 58 000 NEVERS


Engineering: JM GRIMOIN BET Fluid engineering services for Electricity - Smoke extraction - Heating - Ventilation - Air Conditioning - Plumbing
Additional services:
- RT 2005
- SSI





The project in brief:
It aims to
- Bring the existing EHPAD rooms up to standards by increasing their surface area and creating fully accessible bathrooms for disabled or mobility-impaired people.
The nursing home's accommodation capacity is maintained at 81 beds, and the increased room area requires the creation of rooms in a building to be constructed as an extension of the existing buildings.
- Reorganize the functioning of accommodation units
- Create a day reception structure in a building to be created as an extension of the existing buildings
- Reorganize the kitchen / restaurant / relaxation sectors
- Reorganize general services.
